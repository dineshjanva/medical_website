Cardiology Services

Comprehensive heart care for all your cardiovascular needs, from prevention to advanced treatment.

About Our Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by experienced cardiologists who provide comprehensive care for all heart-related conditions. We focus on prevention, early detection, and effective treatment of cardiovascular diseases.

We offer a full spectrum of cardiac services, from non-invasive diagnostics to advanced interventional procedures. Our team works collaboratively to develop personalized treatment plans for each patient, ensuring the best possible outcomes.

Cardiology Services We Offer

Cardiac Diagnostics

ECG, Echocardiography, Stress Tests, Holter Monitoring

Interventional Cardiology

Angioplasty, Stent Placement, Cardiac Catheterization

Preventive Cardiology

Risk Assessment, Lifestyle Counseling, Cholesterol Management

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Supervised Exercise Programs, Education, Psychological Support
